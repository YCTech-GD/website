Our flagship product

Meet recognICE

recognICE is the first real-time community alert mobile app designed to inform communities about the presence of ICE (Immigration and Customs Enforcement) operations in their area.

Built with cutting-edge technology — Flutter, Firebase Firestore, background geolocation — the app provides real-time alerts, ICE detection on your travel route, and a community reporting system where every user contributes to protecting others.

🗺️
Real-time mapVisualize community-reported operations on an interactive map with multiple views: satellite, standard and dark mode.
🔔
150-meter alertsInstant notifications when an ICE operation is within 150 meters of your location, even when the app is closed.
🛣️
Route detectionTrace your route and the app automatically detects ICE operations along your path, alerting you with a red route.
👥
Community feedbackAny user can report and remove operations. Reports automatically expire after 12 hours.
🌐
Bilingual ES / ENFully available in Spanish and English with instant language switching inside the app.
