Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Y&C Tech Global Development ("the Company", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, stores and protects information from users who access and use the recognICE mobile application, available for Android and iOS devices.

By downloading, installing or using the Application, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any of the terms set forth herein, please do not use the Application.

recognICE is a community safety tool. We are committed to collecting only the minimum data necessary to provide our services, and we will never sell or commercialize your personal information.

02

Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

The Application collects geographic location data (latitude and longitude) from the user's device for the following purposes:

  • Map display: To show your position on the real-time map.
  • Proximity alerts: To detect ICE operations within 150 meters of your location and send immediate notifications.
  • Route planning: To trace routes and detect ICE activity along your path.
  • Background monitoring: The app requests always-on location access to send alerts even when the app is closed.

2.2 User-Generated Content

Users may voluntarily report ICE operation locations. This information includes:

  • Geographic coordinates of the reported operation.
  • Optional text description provided by the user.
  • Timestamp (date and time) of the report.

This information is stored in our cloud database and is visible to all users of the Application. Reports are automatically deleted 12 hours after creation.

2.3 Anonymous User Identifier

The Application uses anonymous authentication provided by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC). A unique anonymous identifier (UID) is generated for each device. This identifier is not linked to any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number or other personal data.

2.4 Technical Data

The Application may automatically collect certain technical data necessary for its operation, including device type and operating system, installed application version, and error logs and performance data.

03

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Providing the core features of the Application, including the real-time map and proximity alerts.
  • Sending push notifications when ICE activity is detected near the user.
  • Sharing anonymized report information with other Application users.
  • Improving the performance and functionality of the Application.
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraudulent or abusive use of the Application.

We do NOT use collected information to:

  • Sell, rent or commercialize personal data to third parties.
  • Serve targeted or personalized advertising.
  • Create identifiable user profiles.
  • Any purpose other than those expressly stated in this Policy.
04

Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase, a Google LLC service, for cloud storage and real-time synchronization of ICE activity reports (Firebase Firestore), and anonymous user authentication (Firebase Authentication). Use of Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4.2 OpenStreetMap & OSRM

The Application uses OpenStreetMap for map visualization and the OSRM service for route calculation. These services may receive geographic coordinates to process route requests. Their use is subject to their respective policies.

4.3 Nominatim

The Application uses the Nominatim geocoding service from OpenStreetMap for place searches. Search queries are sent to Nominatim servers for processing.

05

Data Storage & Security

Data stored in our database is protected by the security measures provided by Firebase/Google Cloud, including encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.

ICE activity reports are automatically deleted after 12 hours. We do not store historical location data from users.

Despite the security measures implemented, no electronic transmission or storage system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted through the Application.

Your Rights

In accordance with applicable data protection laws, users have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about data collected associated with your device.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of data associated with your anonymous identifier.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the means indicated in Section 10 of this document.

The Application is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your minor child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take the necessary steps.

08

App Permissions

Android

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: To obtain the user's location.
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: To monitor location in the background and send alerts when the app is closed.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send alert notifications to the user.
  • INTERNET: To communicate with Firebase servers and mapping services.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To keep the background monitoring service active.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To restart the monitoring service when the device is turned on.

iOS

  • NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription: Location while the app is in use.
  • NSLocationAlwaysAndWhenInUseUsageDescription: Background location for continuous alerts.
